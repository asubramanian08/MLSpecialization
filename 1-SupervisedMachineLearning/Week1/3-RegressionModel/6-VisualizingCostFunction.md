* Recap
    * model: $f_{w,b}(x)=wx+b$
    * parameters: $w,b$
    * cost function: $J(w,b)=\frac{1}{{2m}}\sum_{i=1}^m(f_{w,b}(x^{(i)})-y^{(i)})^2$
    * Objective: $\min_{w,b} J(s,b)$
